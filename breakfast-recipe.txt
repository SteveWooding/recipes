Ingredients:
 - 1 cup oats
 - a sprinkle of raisins
 - 4 almonds
 - 1 banana
 - 100 ml of milk
 - teaspoon of honey
 - four raspberries
 
Directions:
 1. Place the oats in a cereal bowl.
 
 2. Sprinkle the raisins on.
 
 3. Put the almonds in the bowl.
 
 4. Peal and slice the banana, placing the slices on top.
 
 5. Pour the milk on.
 
 6. Microwave on full power for around a minute.

 7. Add raspberries.
 
 8. Add honey as a sweetener to your taste.
 
 9. Enjoy!
